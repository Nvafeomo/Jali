2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only to:

  • Provide the service — store your tree, display your family members, play back voice memos, generate transcripts
  • Maintain your account — authentication, password resets, email notifications you've opted into
  • Improve Jali — understand which features are used and where users get stuck, using aggregated and anonymized data
  • Process billing — manage your subscription via Stripe
  • Communicate with you — product updates, policy changes, and responses to your support requests
  • Ensure security — detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access

We do not use your personal information or family data for any other purpose without your explicit consent.

3. What We Don't Do With Your Data

We do not sell your family data. Full stop. This includes voice memos, photos, family relationships, biographical text, and any other content you add to Jali.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to data brokers or marketers
  • We do not use your family tree content to serve you targeted advertising
  • We do not share your data with insurance companies, employers, or government agencies unless legally compelled
  • We do not use your voice memos or photos to train AI or machine learning models
  • We do not share your family history with other Jali users who are not your invited collaborators
  • We do not combine your data with third-party data to build a profile for advertising purposes

5. Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers in the United States. We use industry-standard security practices including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit
  • Hashed passwords (never stored in plaintext)
  • Access controls limiting which team members can access production data
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure

No system is perfectly secure. If we become aware of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you promptly and take steps to contain and investigate it.

6. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Your family tree data, voice memos, and photos are permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Account metadata (email, billing history) may be retained for up to 90 days for legal and accounting purposes
  • Backups containing your data are purged on a rolling basis within 90 days

You can request early deletion of all your data by contacting us at nvafeomo05@gmail.com.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights over your data, regardless of where you live:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — Delete your account and all your data at any time
  • Export — Download your full family archive (paid subscribers get a structured ZIP; free users can export individual profiles)
  • Portability — Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — Object to processing of your data where we rely on legitimate interest

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at nvafeomo05@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is sold or disclosed and to opt out of such sales. We do not sell personal information, so there is nothing to opt out of — but you are entitled to this disclosure.

EU/UK Residents (GDPR / UK GDPR)

If you are located in the EU or UK, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Our lawful basis for processing your data is performance of contract (to provide the service you signed up for) and legitimate interest (security and service improvement).

8. Children's Privacy

Jali accounts are for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

Note: Jali allows you to add children and minors as family members within your tree. This is profile information you enter, not accounts created by those children. Please use discretion when adding information about living minors.

9. Cookies and Tracking

Jali uses minimal cookies:

  • Authentication cookies — to keep you logged in. These are strictly necessary and cannot be disabled without breaking the service.
  • Session cookies — temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies, social media tracking pixels, or cross-site tracking of any kind.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy — particularly changes that affect how we use or share your data — we will notify you by email and display a notice in the app at least 14 days before the changes take effect.

Minor changes (like clarifying language or fixing typos) may be made without notice but will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
